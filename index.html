Why Choose Renewvia Solar Africa

Proven Impact

Our peer-reviewed research shows mini-grids quadruple median income, increase female employment, and boost school attendance for young girls. With 25 operational mini-grids, we deliver measurable community transformation.

Technical Excellence

We built one of East Africa's largest mini-grids at Kalobeyei Refugee Settlement (541 kWp, 1.1 MWh). Our Rural Microgrid Development Model scales reliable solar power to areas unserved by conventional utilities.

Market Leadership

Currently installing 50 solar systems across United Bank of Africa branches in Nigeria. As a certified B Corp, we're expanding C&I solar solutions while maintaining our commitment to community impact.

Measurable Community Impact

We published the first comprehensive, peer-reviewed socioeconomic impact analysis of solar mini-grids in rural Africa. Our research demonstrates that reliable electricity is truly a foundational element for economic growth.

  • Quadrupled median income of Kenyan customers
  • Increased female employment by 17%
  • 20 more young girls attending school per $50,000 invested
  • New businesses and expanded operations
  • Extended study hours for children
