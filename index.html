Rural Mini-grids

Standalone solar photovoltaic and battery-powered plants for communities unserved by conventional utilities or ineligible for grid extension.

Commercial & Industrial Solar

Cost-effective solar solutions for businesses looking to reduce power costs and carbon footprint. Currently expanding across Nigeria with UBA partnership.

Refugee Settlement Power

Specialized large-scale installations for humanitarian contexts, including our flagship 541-kWp system at Kalobeyei Refugee Settlement.

Carbon Asset Platform

Blockchain-based carbon asset platform for mini-grids increases mini-grid revenues by 10%, bringing additional capital flows to communities.

Scalable Energy Solutions

Our Rural Microgrid Development and Operation Model is designed to scale reliable power across Africa. We construct standalone power systems that operate autonomously, accommodating additional capacity from wind or other electricity sources.

In addition to rural electrification, we're rapidly expanding our Commercial & Industrial solar portfolio. With 50 new installations underway at United Bank of Africa branches across Nigeria, we're establishing a strong presence in Africa's growing C&I solar market.

  • Autonomous operation independent of main grid
  • Hybrid solar, battery, and wind capability
  • Proven scalability across diverse markets
  • Commercial-grade reliability and performance
